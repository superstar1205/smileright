Sending Treatment Plans

Paper is a thing of the past, it’s time to go digital! We have made thing easy with just a few clicks you will be communicating digitally in no time.

What do I need to do for my patients?

It's essential you send digital treatment plans to ALL your patients, not just ones who might want finance. Why should only some patients get the digital experience, informed consent materials and communication tools, while others get a boring "black and white" document?

Remember, Smile Right is about patient communication and education, not just finance. We've put together a checklist to help you communicate digital treatment from start to finish:

done

Prepare Patient in Portal - Your patients should be already imported into your portal, if not visit the previous step to see how. Is your patient new? No problem! All you need is a name, mobile and email to communicate.

feed play_circle_filled
done

After Consultation - Once your patient has had their consultation, complete the invitation process and press send, and the job is done.

feed play_circle_filled
done

Send Treatment in 30 Seconds - Save more time by using treatment templates. These prepopulate an offer for Treatment including price, images, videos, informed consent material and more. Use one provided by Smile Right or clone and modify to suit your practice.

feed play_circle_filled
done

Keep Track of Invitations - Sit back and get ready - your patients will start communicating with you electronically via the platform. This includes confirmation on treatment, queries, messages, profile, and health history updates etc., be sure to stay updated. You can check the status on each invitation by going to Invitations > Treatment, here you can see a colour coded and detail view of all your treatments. You also have the bell notification to help you track everything that happens on your portal. Finally, you will receive email notifications of all activity on the platform.

Getting Paid

Provided treatment? Now it’s time to get paid by putting in a settlement request.

Settlement Request

Once treatment has been provided, you can request a settlement for the procedure amount and what you have invoiced your patient. There is no limit to the number of requests you can make.

Your practice manager or the treatment coordinator/receptionist will complete this process as it requires a few things from the patient before they leave.

Sound like a job for you instead?

Click the button below for settlement request instructions or alternatively visit the help centre.

Helpful Tools

zoom_in

Product Lookup: Used to search for all the supplier products available on the platform and will display any information relating to the product selected, including supporting documents, images and videos. Search by Item Code, brand or product. Can also be used for a variety of things, including research, staff training, patient presentation, invitation templates, treatment planning and more!

Financial Calculator: This is a helpful feature! If a patient is enquiring about payment options, costs or repayments, use this feature to show/tell your patient and answer any query they might have on Smile Right payment options. Input the treatment value, and the calculator will show all options available.

attach_money
notifications

Activity Bell: Every action or interaction either by a team member or patient that happens on the platform will give you a notification. It would help if you viewed these notifications regularly to stay up to date with what is happening. You can mark notifications as read, or "star" them if they are essential and return to them later. To view a full audit trail, click the communications tab in the blue bar and see activities and communication to and from the platform.
